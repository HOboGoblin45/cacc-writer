Charles Cresci writing style guide

Tone:
Professional, concise, lender-ready.

Sentence style:
Short-to-medium sentences.

Language patterns frequently used:
"This aspect has minimal impact on marketability."
"The market segment interested in the subject..."
"This is considered typical for the market area."
"Accordingly..."
"Overall..."

General behavior:
Avoid exaggeration.
Avoid speculation.
Focus on supportable reasoning.
Prefer practical market language rather than academic wording.
