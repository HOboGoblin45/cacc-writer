You are writing the MARKET CONDITIONS ANALYSIS section of a UAD 3.6 URAR.

This section replaces the legacy Market Conditions Addendum (Form 1004MC) and must include UAD 3.6 structured data metrics.

REQUIRED STRUCTURED DATA ELEMENTS (ALL must be addressed):
1. Appreciation Rate (% per year)
   - Current appreciation rate or rate of change
   - Over what timeframe? (past 6 months, 12 months, 3 years, etc.)
   - Example: "Property values in the subject market have appreciated at an average rate of 3.5% annually over the past 12 months."

2. Median Days on Market (DOM)
   - Typical marketing time for properties in the subject market
   - Recent median? (last 6 months, 12 months?)
   - Example: "The median days on market for comparable properties is currently 28 days."

3. List-to-Sale Ratio
   - Current ratio (e.g., 98%, 1.02, 95.5%)
   - What does this indicate about market balance?
   - Example: "The list-to-sale ratio is currently 97%, indicating a buyer's market with modest negotiating leverage for sellers."

4. Months Supply of Inventory
   - Current months supply (e.g., "3.2 months supply")
   - Is this a buyer's market (6+ months), balanced market (3-6 months), or seller's market (less than 3 months)?
   - Example: "Current inventory represents approximately 4.5 months of supply at current absorption rates, indicating a balanced market."

5. Absorption Rate
   - Rate of property sales per month or per quarter (if available)
   - Example: "Market absorption rate is approximately 2.3% per month."

NARRATIVE CONTENT:
6. Market Trend Analysis
   - Overall trend: stable, appreciating, depreciating
   - Direction of change (improving, worsening, unchanged)
   - Factors driving market trend (economic conditions, employment, population growth, etc.)

7. Supply and Demand Analysis
   - Is supply constrained or abundant?
   - Demand factors: buyer interest, local employment, affordability
   - Impact on pricing and marketability

8. Financing and Interest Rate Environment
   - Are fixed-rate and ARM financing readily available?
   - Recent trend in interest rates (increasing, decreasing, stable)
   - Impact on buyer pool

9. Market Appeal and Buyer Pool
   - Neighborhood appeal to buyers
   - Target buyer demographic
   - Demand for property type in this market

10. Typical Seller Concessions
    - Are seller-paid concessions typical in this market?
    - Range of typical concessions (if known)
    - Impact of concessions on pricing

11. Forecast/Outlook (if appropriate)
    - Short-term market outlook (next 3-6 months)
    - Any anticipated changes to market conditions

CRITICAL RULES:
- MUST include ALL UAD metrics: appreciation rate, median DOM, list-to-sale ratio, inventory months, absorption rate
- Use specific percentages and numbers — NOT vague terms like "strong demand" or "stable market"
- Do NOT invent metrics. Use [INSERT market metric] if data is unavailable.
- Support conclusions with specific facts from the data provided
- Professional appraisal voice, factual and technical
- Keep to 6-8 paragraphs
