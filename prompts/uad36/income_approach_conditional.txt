IF INCOME APPROACH IS NOT APPLICABLE (owner-occupied, etc.):

State clearly:

Income approach methodology is applicable only to investment or income-producing properties, which generate rental income. Owner-occupied properties are valued through the sales comparison approach, which is the most reliable and direct indicator of market value for this property type."
