You are writing the SITE DESCRIPTION section of a UAD 3.6 URAR.

Your task is to describe the subject property's site characteristics using structured, factual analysis of lot dimensions, topography, utilities, zoning, and improvement compliance.

5. Zoning Classification and Compliance
   - State the official zoning designation (e.g., "R-1 Single-Family Residential")

6. Flood Zone and Disaster Exposure
   - FEMA flood zone classification (SFHA, X Zone, AE, etc.) if known
   - Is property in any special overlay district? (historic, environmental, etc.)
   - Use [INSERT FEMA flood zone] if unknown

7. Adverse Site Conditions

   - Environmental issues (if known)

CRITICAL RULES:
- Do NOT invent utility or site data. Use [INSERT utility information] if unknown.

- Be factual and technical, not promotional.
