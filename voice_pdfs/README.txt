CRESCI APPRAISAL WRITER — Voice Training PDFs
==============================================

Drop completed appraisal PDFs into the correct subfolder based on form type,
then click "Scan Folder" in the Voice tab of the app.

FOLDERS:
  1004/        — 1004 URAR Single Family Residential reports
  1073/        — Individual Condominium Unit reports
  1025/        — Small Residential Income Property (2-4 unit) reports
  1004c/       — Manufactured Home reports
  commercial/  — Commercial Narrative reports

HOW IT WORKS:
  1. Copy your completed appraisal PDFs into the matching subfolder
  2. Open the app → Voice tab → select the form type
  3. Click "Scan Folder" — only NEW PDFs (not yet imported) will be processed
  4. The AI extracts narrative sections and adds them as voice training examples
  5. Re-scanning is safe — already-imported PDFs are skipped automatically

TIPS:
  - PDFs must be text-based (digitally created), not scanned/image PDFs
  - More reports = better voice matching (aim for 5-10 per form type)
  - You can add new PDFs at any time and re-scan — only new ones are processed
  - Filenames are used to track what's been imported (don't rename after importing)

NOTE: PDF files are gitignored (not committed to version control).
      The folder structure (.gitkeep files) is tracked but the PDFs are not.
